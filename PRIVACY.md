# Privacy Policy for AI Skipper

Last updated: January 04, 2026

AI Skipper ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to the AI Skipper Chrome Extension.

## 1. Data Collection
**AI Skipper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** 
- All settings, keywords, and blocklists are stored locally on your device using the `chrome.storage.local` API.
- We do not have access to your browsing history, account information, or any other personally identifiable information.

## 2. Data Usage
The extension only processes video metadata (titles, descriptions, channel names) locally on your machine to determine if a video should be skipped based on your custom configuration. No data is ever sent to external servers.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, we do not share any data with third parties.

## 4. Permissions
The extension requests the following permissions for the sole purpose of functionality:
- `storage`: To save your preferences locally.
- `host permissions (*://*.youtube.com/*)`: To function on the YouTube website.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
